Why Try Sailing?

This website will help young adults and people who have been sailing for a while but haven’t found the right spot or supplies for sailing. We want people who know nothing about sailing to be able to learn enough to get started, and we want to be able to teach an old dog a new trick with some information about sailing, whether it’s a new place to sail, or new tips and tricks! This is for people from Rexburg all the way to Twin Falls and everywhere in between!

All The Information You Need!

This website will have maps to show you the best places to go sailing for different circumstances and have videos of how to sail. This is the only website to have all this updated information about sailing in South-East Idaho that instructs beginner sailors how to sail as well as providing maps and other resources for experienced sailors to sail safely. Our goal is to have a website for People from south-east Idaho, where you can find all the information in one spot for sailing, this includes locations of where to sail, where to rent/purchase a sailboat, how to sail safely, optional gear for your trip, and more advanced tips for when you have been sailing for a while. We are your one stop shop for all things sailing in south-east Idaho!
