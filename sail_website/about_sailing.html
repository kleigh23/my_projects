Did You Know?

Sailing has been a part of the human experience for century’s, in fact some of the earliest art ever discovered pictured sailboats. Every major civilization sailed and even had their own unique sail boats. Some of these are familiar to you, the Viking longboat, the Polynesian Voyaging Canoe, and of course the Spanish Galleon.

Tradition

The traditions of sailing are still strong in the coastal regions of most countries, but with the advance of transportation sailing is spreading inland. Now sailing is a common sight on some of Idaho’s lakes and reservoirs. You may have noticed driving past American Falls reservoir some large colorful sails skimming the water, just a few years ago that would have seemed out of place, but today it is a more common occurrence.
